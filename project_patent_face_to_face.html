FACE-TO-FACE INTERACTION DEVICE, WEARABLE DEVICE USING SAME, AND INTERACTION FACILITATION METHOD

Abstract

This patent, issued on January 4, 2018, describes a device designed to enhance face-to-face interactions. The invention includes a wearable device and a method to facilitate interaction by detecting and responding to the presence of another individual in proximity.

Patent Details

  • Patent Number: WO/2018/003986
  • Issue Date: January 4, 2018
  • Country: Japan

Technical Features

The device incorporates sensors to detect the presence of nearby individuals and provides visual and auditory cues to facilitate interaction. The wearable design ensures convenience and ease of use in everyday situations.

Applications

The device is useful in various scenarios such as social gatherings, business meetings, educational environments, and healthcare settings. It enhances face-to-face communication by providing real-time feedback and interaction facilitation.

Interaction Facilitation Method

The method involves detecting the presence of another individual, generating appropriate responses, and providing feedback to the user. It utilizes advanced sensors and algorithms to adapt to different contexts and improve interaction quality.

Conclusion

This invention significantly advances interpersonal communication technology by facilitating more effective face-to-face interactions, improving social dynamics, and communication efficiency in various settings.
